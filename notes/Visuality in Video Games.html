Visuality in Video Games

There are different types of images to be found in and around video games.

  • the image during play1, what is mostly centering around the mediated space in Nitsche
  • the image of play2, such as screenshots, in-game photography or screencasts
  • paratextual visual material3, such as box art, manuals or other ephemera
