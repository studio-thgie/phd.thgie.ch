Assemblage

“DeLanda defines assemblages as ‘wholes whose properties emerge from the interactions between parts’.” (Guay-Bélanger, 2022, p. 668)

Assemblages have kinship with the concept of intertextuality. An Assemblage is fluid. It’s part can exist on their own and become parts of other or multiple assemblages. ## Sources - Assembling Auras: Towards a Methodology for the Preservation and Study of Video Games as Cultural Heritage Artefacts
