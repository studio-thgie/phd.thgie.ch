Can you see Switzerland?

Interdisciplinary perspectives on en/decoding nationality in the visuality of early Swiss digital games

This dissertation project inquires into the complexity of ascribing nationality to digital games in early games attributed to Switzerland. The research focus lies on the visuality, the image seen on screen but also the paratextual material. Continue reading the abstract.
